FPV Sampa Battery pack planner

Simple tool to plan battery pack configurations. Just inform the basic pack specs and the tool will suggest the possible shapes of the pack.

The more cells in series, the more voltage of the pack.
The more cells in parallel, the more capacity of the pack.
The nominal capacity of each cell in miliampere-hour (mAh).
The constant discharge rate of each cell in Amperes (A).
The weight of each cell in grams (g).
The nominal voltage of a single cell (V).
